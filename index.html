Career Summary

Since graduating from Dublin City University in 2019 with a BSc Marketing, Innovation and Technology, I have been fortunate to have had the opportunity to work with a new Irish whiskey brand in the New York market. My main responsibility is to increase the awareness of the brand through advocacy and story-telling, by presenting to key target market groups. In order to achieve increased brand awareness, I organise various events where I can showcase the product itself and the story behind the whiskey to members in relevant trade groups like bartenders or end consumer of key target market groups like Irish Americans.

Before graduating with a high 2.1 degree from DCU, I was lucky to receive invaluable hands-on and relevant business experience. From working with Enterprise Ireland for my internship year, to independently conducting research and developing marketing strategies for small Irish businesses in my final year of University. DCU gave me multiple opportunities to apply marketing theory and gain relevant work experience before even graduating.
